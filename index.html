About

A graphic designer and illustrator based in Barcelona

I am a graphic designer, illustrator and visual artist from Argentina, currently based in Barcelona - SPAIN.

Diploma's

Postgrade degree in packaging design from BAU design college of Barcelona (2013) Master degree in graphic design, postgrade diploma in graphic design applied to communication and postgrade diploma in editorial projects from ELISAVA Barcelona school of design (2012). Bachelor degree in graphic design and illustration from ISCV-Rosario, Argentina (2010).
